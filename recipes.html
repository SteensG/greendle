From Our Head Chef
"March is my favourite month to cook. The forced rhubarb is sweet-tart perfection, and wild garlic makes everything sing. This week's menu writes itself."
Elena Marchetti
Head of Recipe Development · Greendle

Elena trained at Ballymaloe Cookery School and spent six years cooking at farm-to-table restaurants in Tuscany and London before joining Greendle. Her philosophy: let what's in season lead, never the other way around.

She visits three or four farms a month to stay close to what's actually growing. "I don't write a recipe until I've tasted the ingredient fresh," she says. "A carrot in October is a completely different vegetable from a carrot in May."
