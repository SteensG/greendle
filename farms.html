Our Process

How we vet every partner

🔍
On-Site Audits

Annual third-party inspections of every farm, no exceptions. Our sourcing team also makes at least one unannounced visit per year per partner.

📋
Fair Price Agreement

Every partner receives a guaranteed minimum price above the market rate, locked in for the full season — so farmers can plan, invest, and sleep soundly.

🌱
Regenerative Practices

Soil health, water use, and biodiversity benchmarks are set and reviewed annually. Farms improving scores receive Greendle's Regenerative Partner badge.

🍃

All 180 partners have been visited in person by our sourcing team. We publish the full audit results each year in our Impact Report.
