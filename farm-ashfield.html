Farm Story
"The day Greendle called, we'd just decided to stop supplying supermarkets. Best timing of our lives."
Priya & Daniel Nair
Ashfield Organics · Gloucestershire

Priya and Daniel Nair took over the Ashfield plot from Priya's parents in 2016. The land — 22 acres of Cotswold brash and clay loam in the foothills above Stroud — had been farmed by her family since her grandparents arrived from Kerala in the late 1960s. Priya studied agroecology at Bristol; Daniel came from a background in logistics and supply chain.

For four years they sold through a regional wholesaler. The prices were unpredictable, the feedback was zero, and the food was travelling fifty miles in the wrong direction before reaching anyone's plate. In spring 2020, Greendle's sourcing team knocked on their gate during a routine Gloucestershire scouting trip. Priya nearly turned them away. She hadn't heard of us.

Today 100% of Ashfield's harvest goes directly into Greendle subscriber boxes. Priya says it's the first time she's been able to plan more than one season ahead.
